# Branding Guide

## Core Identity

Primary brand: `From Algorithms to Autonomous Systems`

Tagline: `Engineering Intelligence at Scale`

## Voice

- Clear, technical, and product-minded
- Senior engineer tone, not student tone
- Short sentences with concrete outcomes

## Visual System

- Dark base, cyan/mint/amber accents
- Glass cards, subtle glow, compact spacing
- SaaS-style diagrams and dashboard surfaces

## Content Rules

- Lead with proof, not biography
- Prefer screenshots and metrics over paragraphs
- Remove filler, hedging, and repeated claims
